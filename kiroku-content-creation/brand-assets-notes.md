## General notes on image use

- Do not use logos without explicit permission, even if found publicly online
- Do not use photographs of individuals without confirmation that they consented to be identified in published content
- Archive images (ship plans, documents) may be subject to copyright held by the originating institution — verify before use
- Where images are used, include a proper credit line and source
